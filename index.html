Our Purpose

Bridging Language & Knowledge

Islamic scholarship has been preserved in classical Arabic texts for centuries. For non-Arabic speakers, accessing this sacred knowledge can feel overwhelming—scattered PDFs, confusing notes, and no clear path forward.

Mutoon exists to change that. We create structured, interactive workbooks that transform classical texts into accessible learning tools. Our workbooks don't just translate—they guide you through the material with spaces to write, reflect, and truly engage with the knowledge.

We believe that language doesn't need to be a barrier between a student and their religion. With Mutoon, you can organically study Islamic sciences at your own pace, building a personal library of knowledge that becomes part of your journey toward Allah.

Authenticity & Credibility

Our commitment to authenticity is absolute. We do not add anything from ourselves. Every piece of content in our workbooks is taken directly from official websites of the scholars (shuyookh), only reformatted for your convenience. We serve as a bridge, not an interpreter—preserving the integrity of the original teachings while making them accessible to English-speaking students.

How Our Workbooks Work

Each Mutoon workbook is meticulously designed to provide an immersive and effective learning experience:

  • Color-Coded Side-by-Side Format: Arabic matn alongside English translation, making it easy to follow and learn both languages simultaneously
  • Structured Notebook Sections: The text is divided into clear sections with dedicated space to write notes under each subpoint
  • QR Codes to Relevant Lessons: Quick access to audio/video lessons from scholars to deepen your understanding
  • Table of Keywords: Essential vocabulary and terms for quick reference and memorization
  • Space for Personal Summaries: Create your own reflections and summaries to solidify your learning
